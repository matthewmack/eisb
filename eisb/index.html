Plan News

  • Published: 04/25/2016 Annual Funding Notice
    This notice includes important information about the funding status of your pension plan ("the Plan") and general information about the benefit payments guaranteed by the Pension Benefit Guaranty Corporation ("PBGC"), a federal insurance agency.
  • Published: 03/30/2016 Performance Reports for NCEW Pension and SFEW RSP
    The 4th quarter investment performance reports are now available for the San Francisco Electrical Workers Retirement Savings Plan and for the Northern California Electrical Workers Pension Trust.
  • Published: 03/29/2016 IBEW LOCAL UNION 6/SFECA is sponsoring a scholarship program!
    This year the IBEW Local 6/SFECA Scholarship Committee will award a total of fifteen educational grants to sons and daughters of contributors to EISB, Inc. including office staff and IBEW Local 6 members who are working for contributing employers (including sons and daughters of deceased members who were working for contributing employers at the time of their death). The Scholarship will be awarded based on merit according to a selection criteria that will include an essay and an individual interview.
  • Published: 11/12/2015 Notice Regarding Increase to Retiree Monthly Copayments
    Each year the Trustees are presented with a report from the Plan Consultants on adjustments to the retiree co-payments. The following table includes the adjusted monthly rates that will take effect on February 1, 2016.
  • Published: 11/05/2015 401(k) Election and Compensation Reduction Agreement for 2016
    Use this to wish to make a 401(k) deferral from 2016 hourly wages to your account in the San Francisco Electrical Workers Retirement Savings Plan.
  • Published: 11/05/2015 2016 Open Enrollment for Wage Deferral Election
    All employees who have attained at least 45% Apprentice level and are working for an employer that contributes to the San Francisco Electrical Workers Retirement Savings Plan, may elect (or revise their current election) to make 401(k) pre-tax wage deferrals during open enrollment.
  • Published: 10/07/2015 OptumRx and Catamaran Combine
    OptumRx® and Catamaran® have joined forces to deliver enhanced pharmacy benefit services and a better health care experience for members.
  • Published: 09/01/2015 Fidelity Investment Participant Disclosure Notice-Retirement Savings Plan
    This Participant Disclosure Notice is being provided as required by federal law to those who are eligible to participate or have a balance in the San Francisco Electrical Workers Retirement Savings Plan (the "Plan") and have the right to direct investments.
  • Published: 05/27/2015 Notice to all Employers and Local Union 6 Inside Wiremen
    Year 2014 contract negotiations settlement wage fringe packet.
  • Published: 03/24/2015 IBEW LOCAL UNION 6/SFECA is sponsoring a scholarship program!
    This year the IBEW Local 6/SFECA Scholarship Committee will award a total of fifteen educational grants to sons and daughters of contributors to EISB, Inc. including office staff and IBEW Local 6 members who are working for contributing employers (including sons and daughters of deceased members who were working for contributing employers at the time of their death). The Scholarship will be awarded based on merit according to a selection criteria that will include an essay and an individual interview.
  • Published: 02/02/2015 Benefit Improvements for those Retiring on or After January 1, 2014
    The Board of Trustees of the Northern California Electrical Workers Pension Plan are pleased to advise you of the benefit improvement to the Plan which they adopted at the meeting held on December 9, 2014.
