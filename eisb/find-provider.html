    Find Provider

    To find network (PPO) providers click on any of the links shown below. The search page will appear in a new browser window.

    Hospital and Doctors: Where to Call:

    Self Funded PPO Plan
    To find a provider, simply go to www.blueshieldca.com and:

    1. Click "Find a Provider."
    2. Select "Find a Doctor" or "Find a Hospital or other facilities."
    3. Choose a Plan: In the drop down menu, select "Blue Shield PPO."
    4. Choose a search type and enter criteria; in this section you can select the provider/facility type, specialty or search by name.

    In-State Phone Number:
    1-888-568-3560

    Out-of-State Phone Number:
    1-800-810-BLUE (2583)

    HMO Plan
    To find a provider, simply go to www.blueshieldca.com and:

    1. Click "Find a Provider."
    2. Select "Find a Doctor" or "Find a Hospital or other facilities."
    3. Choose a Plan: In the drop down menu, select "HMO+Access."
    4. Choose a search type and enter criteria; in this section you can select the provider/facility type, specialty or search by name.

    Phone Number: 1-800-303-5921

    Kaiser Foundation Health Plan (HMO)
    Kaiser Foundation is alternate to the Self Funded PPO Plan.
